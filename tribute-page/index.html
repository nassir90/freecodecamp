Tribute To Neil Postman

Neil Postman wrote about media epistemology. He argued that the form of intellegence promonted by a medium is that which is required to consume this medium. It follows as a corollary from his theory that instant media such as the televison doesn't encourage much intellegence at all!

Criticisms

Despite compelling arguments on the part of Neil Postman, some would argue that the resason that society is degenerating is not a matter of transient factors such as forms of media transmission and collection, but demographics. Put simply, all human traits such as intellegence fall somewhere on a bell curve, and this is genetic rather than epigenetic. Therefore, some would say that if social groups with lower median intellegence and/or more maladaptive traits per capita are growing in number, in tandem with the rise of these "instant media" it is likeley the former that is to blame for a degenerating society
